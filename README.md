## 📋 Project Overview
This project involves a deep-dive analysis of a digital music store's database. By writing structured SQL queries, I've extracted actionable insights regarding **business growth**, **customer behavior**, and **sales performance**. 

The goal is to help the store optimize its marketing strategies and understand its top-performing regions and customers.

---

## 🗄️ Database Schema
The dataset is structured to provide a 360-degree view of the business, containing:
* 🎤 **Artists & Albums:** Track-level data and creator info.
* 🎧 **Tracks & Playlists:** Detailed music inventory.
* 👥 **Customers & Invoices:** Transactional data and billing details.
* 👔 **Employees:** Organizational hierarchy and management.

---

## 🎯 Key Business Questions Answered
I solved several business problems using SQL aggregation, joins, and filtering:

1.  **Seniority Analysis:** Identified the senior-most employee based on job title.
2.  **Market Presence:** Found the countries with the highest number of invoices to pinpoint top-performing markets.
3.  **Revenue Tracking:** Identified the top 3 highest invoice values.
4.  **Event Planning:** Determined the city with the highest revenue to suggest the best location for a **Promotional Music Festival**.
5.  **Customer Loyalty:** Identified the "Best Customer" (the person who spent the most money).
